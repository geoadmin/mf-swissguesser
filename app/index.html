Welcome to SwissGuesser, the game that takes you on a historical trip through the country.

First we will show you a photograph from the Swiss Federal Archives, dating from at least 50 years ago, taken somewhere in Switzerland. Look at the picture (click to zoom full screen), and think about where it was taken. Then click anywhere on the map to place your guess.

The closer you are to the actual location, the more points you'll get.
